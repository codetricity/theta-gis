Starting Video

The video for most file formats can be started through the API. 8K video cannot be started with the API. You can use a bluetooth clicker.

  • Bluetooth Clicker Operates at 8K video at 2, 5, 10 fps video (Joby Impulse. There is also official RICOH bluetooth clicker)
  • 2 hour long 8K and 5.7K videos
